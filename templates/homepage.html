About Us

Balozi Electrical and General Supplies is a company founded on 2002 and has been operational since then, bringing several projects to completion to our customers' satisfaction. It was founded by Mr. Fanuel Ayuyo Ambuli who has had extensive experience in the construction industry for over 30 years. We specialize in a wide range of construction services, including project management, general contracting, office partitioning design, gypsum installation, home interior design, painting services, renovations and remodeling, floor installation, extensions and additions, plumbing and electrical installations, and finishing services. What started as a small venture has now grown into a reputable construction company known for its commitment to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Over the years we have worked with several highly reputable institutions such as Population Services Kenya, Troicare, Population Services International, Atlantis, Electrical installations and maintenance at industrial go-downs along Mombasa road and church based organisations such as Deliverance Church Karen just to mention a few.
